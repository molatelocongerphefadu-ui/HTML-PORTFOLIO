My top 3 movies of all-time

Dumb and the Dumber

In terms of comedy impact, Dumb and Dumber is one of the greatest slapstick comedies of all time. It’s endlessly rewatchable, wildly quotable, and fearless in how stupid it’s willing to be. It doesn’t try to be clever—it accidentally becomes iconic by being perfectly, proudly dumb.

Top Gun-Maverick

What makes Maverick truly great, though, is its soul. The relationship with Rooster (Goose’s son) adds real emotional gravity. The film balances nostalgia with growth, proving that it respects the past without being trapped by it. When old themes and music return, they don’t feel reused—they feel earned, powerful, and deeply emotional.
